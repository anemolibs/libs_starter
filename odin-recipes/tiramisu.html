Tiramisu Graham Ref Cake



Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian no-bake dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and incredible mascarpone cream.

The custard-like cream is excellent and contains no raw egg. This easy recipe is truly the best homemade tiramisu and always gets rave reviews.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Nescafe Classic
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 10 pieces Graham crackers, crushed
  • 1/2 bar cream cheese
  • 1 packet 250ml Nestle All-purpose cream
  • 1/3 cup Nestle Carnation condensada
  • 3 tablespoons Milo powder

Steps

  1. Dissolve NESCAFÉ Classic and sugar in hot water. (2mins)
  2. Whip cream cheese in a bowl until light. Add NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream and continue to whip. Pour NESTLÉ® Carnation® Condensada and whip until light and smooth. (8mins)
  3. Wash and dry banana leaf, if using, and line a 9-inch cast-iron skillet with it. (One leaf should be enough, but if more are needed, make sure to overlap leaves so there are no gaps.)
  4. Dip graham cracker in coffee mixture. Assemble in layers in a container with cream mixture. Repeat until layers are formed. (10mins)
  5. Refrigerate overnight.
  6. Top with MILO® powder and serve well chilled. (2mins)
